A half-decent life

Description:

The recipe for a decent life is rather difficult. Only skilled chefs are able to pull it off.

Ingredients:

  • Family/Friends (sometimes they are interchangeable)
  • Love (and I mean platonic love - being in a relationship is NOT required for 100% completion)
  • A LOT of money (they say money isn't everything, but have you seen the price of rent?)
  • Onions, Bell Peppers, Mushrooms, etc.

Steps:

  1. Be present.
  2. Work hard.
  3. Try not to get distracted.

And that's about it! Enjoy!
