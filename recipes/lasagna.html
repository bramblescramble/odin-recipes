Lasagna... Garfield's Best Friend

Description:

Lasagna, a classic dish. Made with flat layers of pasta with sauce, meat, veggies, and cheese betwixt. A mouth-watering, simple recipe which is sure to please just about anyone. Omit the meat if you are a vegetarian/vegan.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Onions, Bell Peppers, Mushrooms, etc.

Steps:

  1. Take the pasta and boil it.
  2. Cook the meat and veggies.
  3. Alternate putting a layer of pasta and a layer of sauce/meat/cheese/etc.
  4. Top with more cheese.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven for awhile. Wanna know numbers? Consult an actual cook!
  6. Let it cool down and then serve it.

And that's about it! Enjoy!
