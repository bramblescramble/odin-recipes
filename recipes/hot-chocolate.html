Hot Chocolate, the opposite of Cold Vanilla

Description:

Hot choco.... A warm hug on a cold day, or a sweaty hug on a hot day. Your choice!

Ingredients:

  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Water or Milk
  • (optional) Marshmallows

Steps:

  1. Put the powdered product into a mug.
  2. Pour in the boiling liquid.
  3. That's it, it's really not hard to grasp

And that's about it! Enjoy!
